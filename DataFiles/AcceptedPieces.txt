+”The Starry Night” is an oil-on-canvas painting by Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh. It is inspired by his view in the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum. It is subject to many interpretations ranging from religion to emotional turmoil. It has been housed in the Museum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City since 1941.+

+”Untitled” is a spray paint, acrylic, and oilstick painting on canvas by American artist Jean-Michel Basquiat. It was sold 3 times before it ended up in a private collection, only being shown in certain exhibitions. It was the first artwork created after 1980 to sell for more than $100,000,000 and he was the youngest artist to eclipse that mark at 21 years old.+
