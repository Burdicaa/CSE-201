+”Mona Lisa” is an oil painting on white poplar panel by Italian artist Leonardo da Vinci. It is believed that the painting is of the Italian noblewoman Lisa del Giocondo. It is one of the most valuable paintings in the world. It has been housed in The Louvre Museum in Paris since 1797 (besides when it was stolen from 1911-1914).+
